About Me

Material, memory, and movement

Damien Hohn is a multidisciplinary artist born in Red Hook, Brooklyn, New York. His practice combines painting, collage, and found urban textures, examining how places shape personal memory and collective identity.

In each series, Damien builds layered surfaces that invite viewers to move closer, discovering subtle marks and hidden narratives over time.
